dwtmode

Discrete Wavelet Transform Extension Mode

Parameters

mode:

extension mode, 'symh'('sym'), 'symw', 'asymh', 'asymw', 'zpd', 'zpd', 'per', 'ppd'.

Description

dwtmode is to display or change extension mode.

Examples

dwtmode;
dwtmode('zpd');
